ChzBurger Phx: The Ultimate Smash

📍 123 Washington St, Phoenix, AZ 💰 Price: $$ ★★★★★

⚡ Quick Take

The Vibe: Neon-lit, fast-casual walkup counter with loud hip-hop playing.

Best For: A messy, indulgent late-night bite after drinks downtown.

Standout: The lacey, crispy edges on their smash patties are unmatched anywhere in the State.

The Review

For years, the Phoenix burger scene has been dominated by old-school taverns or massive, towering creations that you have to eat with a fork and knife. But the smash burger revolution has finally taken over the Valley, and sitting right at the absolute pinnacle is ChzBurger Phx.

I arrived on a Friday night around 9 PM. The line was out the door and wrapping around the corner of Washington Street, which is usually a bad sign for my patience but a great sign for the food. Once inside, the menu is refreshingly simple. They do one thing here: smash burgers. You choose Single, Double, or Triple.

What to Order

You have to get the Double Smash Meal. The beef is an 80/20 chuck blend that they smash so aggressively onto the flat top that the edges literally turn into a meat lace. It's crispy, deeply deeply savory, and pairs perfectly with the squishy, locally baked potato bun that somehow holds the whole messy affair together.

Don't skip the fries—tossed in a house seasoning that tastes slightly of paprika and garlic powder. Grab an extra side of "Chz Sauce" for dipping.

✅ Pros

  • Best smash patty in Arizona
  • Incredibly fast service despite lines
  • Open until 2 AM on weekends

❌ Cons

  • No indoor seating, only standing counters
  • Parking downtown can be tricky
